Sunday's Sermon
  • David came with the NAME OF THE LORD to defeat Goliath.
  • Paul can do all things THROUGH CHRIST!!
  • sword, spear, javelin - human strength
  • David didn’t count on natural strength but spiritual/supernatural strength.
  • We don’t have to FEEL the strength of God we just have to trust that we have it!
  • You can not have the God of Strength in you and not have His qualities
  • “Isn’t there a cause” 1 Sam 17:29
  • Strength is the quality of being physical strong.
  • When the power of God is on you it ends up being physical and visible.
  • God can multiply our strength!
  • David defeated Goliath with something physical-a stone, not in the spirit.
  • Strength Is the capacity to withstand great force or pressure.
  • God isn’t going to allow me to face a test stronger than Him!
  • Synonyms for strength: Robust, Sturdy, Firm, Unsinkable, Durable...
  • Survivor- one who lives while the other dies.
  • Qualities of the Strong:
    1. Enthusiastic
    2. Trustworthy
    3. Creative
    4. Disciplined
    5. Dedicated
    6. Honest
    7. Punctual
    8. Friendly
    9. Flexible
    10. Mindful
